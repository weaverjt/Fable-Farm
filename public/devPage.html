Fable Farm

Fable Farm is a community writing app and the first of its kind! Our app allows our user base to create a short story together in a way that has never before been attempted! The process begins when a user submits a original idea in a new thread for community review. They will be able to provide the short story's set up or the premise of the character they wish to write about as well as set guidelines for the thread---no clich√©s, PG rating, etc. About us
